# Optimization in Physical Systems

This chapter explores how Physical AI Engineering approaches can improve optimization problems that involve physical systems, combining optimization algorithms with physics-based constraints and models.

## Key Topics

- Physics-constrained optimization
- Topology optimization using neural networks
- Optimal design of physical systems
- Optimization with partial differential equation constraints
- Multi-objective optimization in physical systems
- Real-time optimization for dynamic systems

## Benefits of Physics-Aware Optimization

Incorporating physics knowledge into optimization:
- Reduces dimensionality of search space
- Improves convergence and stability
- Ensures physically feasible solutions
- Accelerates optimization through gradient information
- Provides interpretable solutions

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will:
- Understand how to incorporate physical constraints into optimization
- Know techniques for physics-aware optimization
- Be familiar with applications in engineering design
- Understand the computational benefits of physics-aware optimization
