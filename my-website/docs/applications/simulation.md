# Physics-Based Simulation

This chapter focuses on using Physical AI Engineering for creating more accurate, efficient, and generalizable simulations of physical systems, from simple mechanical systems to complex multiphysics problems.

## Simulation Approaches

- Neural network-accelerated simulation
- Learning discretization schemes
- Surrogate modeling for complex physics
- Uncertainty quantification in simulations
- Multi-scale simulation with AI
- Real-time simulation capabilities

## Benefits of AI-Enhanced Simulation

AI-enhanced simulation offers:
- Faster computation than traditional numerical methods
- Ability to learn from sparse data
- Generalization to new scenarios
- Discovery of reduced-order models
- Improved accuracy in chaotic systems

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will:
- Understand how to enhance physical simulations with AI
- Know techniques for neural network-based simulation
- Be familiar with applications across different physics domains
- Understand the trade-offs between traditional and AI-based simulation
