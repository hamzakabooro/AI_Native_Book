# Robotics Applications

This chapter explores the application of Physical AI Engineering to robotics, where understanding and modeling the physical interaction between robots and their environment is crucial for successful operation.

## Key Application Areas

- Robot dynamics modeling
- Control systems for robotic manipulators
- Legged locomotion
- Multi-robot coordination
- Robot perception and state estimation
- Human-robot interaction

## Physical AI in Robotics

Physical AI techniques are particularly valuable in robotics because:
- Robots operate in the physical world
- Accurate dynamic models are essential for control
- Physical constraints must be respected
- Safety requires understanding of physical interactions

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will:
- Understand how Physical AI applies to robotics
- Know the key challenges in robotic modeling and control
- Be familiar with state-of-the-art techniques in physical robotic systems
- Understand how to integrate physics knowledge into robotic applications
