# Case Study: Fluid Dynamics

This case study explores the application of Physical AI Engineering to fluid dynamics problems, which involve complex partial differential equations (Navier-Stokes) and multi-scale phenomena that challenge traditional modeling approaches.

## Problem Overview

Fluid dynamics encompasses a wide range of phenomena from laminar flow to turbulence. This case study addresses:
- Learning flow fields from sparse measurements
- Surrogate modeling for computational fluid dynamics (CFD)
- Turbulence modeling with neural networks
- Flow control using Physical AI
- Multi-physics coupling with heat transfer

## Methodology

We'll explore various approaches:
1. Physics-informed neural networks for Navier-Stokes equations
2. Neural operators for learning solution mappings
3. Proper orthogonal decomposition with neural networks
4. Lagrangian particle methods with learned interactions
5. Multi-fidelity learning approaches

## Key Learning Points

This case study demonstrates:
- How to handle complex PDEs with neural networks
- Techniques for learning multi-scale phenomena
- Approaches to incorporate physical constraints in fluid problems
- Computational advantages and limitations of learned models

## Learning Objectives

After completing this case study, you will:
- Understand how to apply Physical AI to complex PDE problems
- Know techniques for learning fluid dynamics from data
- Appreciate the challenges in multi-scale physical systems
- Understand the computational aspects of physics-informed CFD
