# Case Study: Robot Control

This case study demonstrates the application of Physical AI Engineering to robot control systems, where understanding and modeling the physical interaction between robots and their environment is crucial for successful operation.

## Problem Overview

Robot control involves managing the dynamics and kinematics of mechanical systems. This case study addresses:
- Learning robot dynamics for model-based control
- Physics-aware control policies
- Handling contact and friction in robotic systems
- Transfer from simulation to reality
- Safe exploration in physical control tasks

## Methodology

We'll explore various approaches:
1. Learning robot dynamics with Lagrangian/Hamiltonian networks
2. Physics-informed reinforcement learning for control
3. Model predictive control with learned dynamics
4. Adaptive control with neural networks
5. Safe control with physical constraints

## Key Learning Points

This case study demonstrates:
- How to incorporate robot physics into learning algorithms
- Techniques for sim-to-real transfer in robotics
- Approaches to ensure safe and stable control
- Benefits of physics-aware control policies

## Learning Objectives

After completing this case study, you will:
- Understand how to apply Physical AI to robotic systems
- Know techniques for learning robot dynamics
- Appreciate the challenges in physical robot control
- Understand safety considerations in Physical AI robotics
