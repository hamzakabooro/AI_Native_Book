# Case Study: Pendulum Modeling

This case study demonstrates the application of Physical AI Engineering concepts to the classic pendulum problem, exploring different approaches to model, control, and analyze pendulum dynamics using neural networks that respect physical laws.

## Problem Overview

The pendulum represents a fundamental dynamical system that exhibits non-linear behavior. This case study explores:
- Learning the dynamics of a simple pendulum
- Modeling a double pendulum with chaotic behavior
- Control of pendulum systems using neural networks
- Comparison of different Physical AI approaches

## Methodology

We'll compare several approaches:
1. Pure neural network models (baseline)
2. Lagrangian Neural Networks
3. Hamiltonian Neural Networks
4. Physics-informed neural networks
5. Neural ODE approaches

## Key Learning Points

This case study illustrates:
- The importance of incorporating physical inductive biases
- How different Physical AI approaches handle chaotic systems
- The trade-offs between accuracy and interpretability
- Techniques for model validation in physical systems

## Learning Objectives

After completing this case study, you will:
- Understand how to apply Physical AI techniques to a simple mechanical system
- Compare different approaches for modeling physical dynamics
- Appreciate the benefits of physics-informed modeling for dynamical systems
- Know how to validate models for physical consistency
